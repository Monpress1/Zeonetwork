Mode of Operation

Membership

Savings

Members must save weekly for 4 weeks (1 month) before becoming eligible for loans. Contributions must be consistent.

Loan Access

Eligible after 4 consecutive weeks of savings. Requires a 10% interest payment upfront. A compulsory 10% of the repayment amount is added to savings during the repayment period.

Repayment

Follows an agreed weekly or daily schedule. A 10% default fee applies to missed payments. Continuous default leads to suspension or deduction from savings.

Record Keeping

Each member maintains a Savings & Loan Card/Record Book. Entries are updated by the Secretary and Treasurer.

Benefits

Access to structured savings, loans after 4 weeks, collective investment opportunities, and financial discipline.

Discipline

False information or rule violations attract penalties. Persistent defaulters face suspension or expulsion.
